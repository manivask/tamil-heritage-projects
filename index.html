Heritage Pioneers & Researchers

🐢

Orissa Balu (ஒரிசா பாலு)

World-renowned Tamil researcher and oceanographer who dedicated his life to researching ancient Tamil maritime history, sea trade routes, and connection to ocean currents.

  • Mapped turtle migration pathways matching ancient Tamil trade routes.
  • Researched "Kumari Kandam" (sunken landmass) and maritime history.
  • Linked names of Tamil origin in East Asia, Africa, and global coastlines.

🌱

G. Nammalvar (கோ. நம்மாழ்வார்)

Pioneer agricultural scientist, organic farming crusader, and environmentalist who fought against chemical agriculture and successfully revived traditional Tamil farming systems.

  • Founded "Vanagam", an ecological crusade center.
  • Rescued and popularized native grain/paddy varieties in Tamil Nadu.
  • Educated thousands of farmers globally in zero-cost natural farming.
